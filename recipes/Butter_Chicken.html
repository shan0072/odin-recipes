Butter Chicken Recipe

Description

Butter chicken is a savory dish that is sure to please even the most discerning curry fanatics. Tender chicken bites suspended in a creamy sauce that combines tomato notes, a hearty texture from yogurt and cocoanut milk, and classic Indian spices make this a dinner time treat!

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken breasts (Qty 4)
  • Tomato paste (8 oz)
  • Fire roasted tomatoes (16 oz)
  • Curry powder (1 Tbsp)
  • Garam Masala (1.5 Tbsp)
  • Cocoanut Milk (1 cup)
  • Diced Yellow Onion (Qty 1)

Recipe

  1. Place the skillet over medium heat and add the olive oil
  2. Gently add the diced onions and stir to coat with the oil. Cook until the onions are soft and tranlucent.
