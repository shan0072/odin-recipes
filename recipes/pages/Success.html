Recipe for Success

Ingredients

  • Positive attitude
  • Creative ideas for solutions or products that will make a difference
  • Tenacious motivation

Recipe

  1. Collect all of the positive attiude
  2. Sort through the ideas and mix one or two with the positive attitude. Note: more than two ideas may cause washout and prevent the finished success from rising
  3. Cultivate the ideas with the tenacious motivation for 3 months. If the ideas have not developed after three months, return to the idea list and pick some new ones
