Parmesan Tilapia Recipe

Description

Parmesan tilapia is an easy recipe that offers the health benefits of fresh fish with a mild flavor profile. Frozen tilapia is a low cost option that is easy to store; however, it often comes with the downside of more "fishy" taste than is acceptable for most. The parmesan flavor masks this fishiness and causes this recipe to land somewhere between the Mediterranean and confort food genres.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons of paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 tilapia filets
  • lemon, cut into wedges

Directions

  1. Preheeat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil
  3. In a shallow bowl, mix together the Parmesan cheese, paprika, parsley, and salt. Drizzle the tilapia with olive oil, then dredge in the cheese mixture, pressing it in lightly with your fingers if necessary.
  4. Bake until the fish is opaque in the thickest part (approximately 10-12 minutes)
